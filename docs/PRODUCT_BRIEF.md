# Product Brief

NagrikSetu is an India public information navigator. It helps citizens, journalists, NGOs, researchers, founders, and civic teams discover public offices, tenders, road/public works responsibility, and grievance paths from one source-first interface.

## Core User Journeys

- Find who to contact for a place, road, department, or issue.
- Find who built or maintains a road or public work.
- Browse tenders by district, department, and work type.
- Raise or route a complaint through the correct official channel.

## MVP Scope

- Search by place, department, road name, tender, or issue.
- Office/contact card.
- Tender card.
- Complaint and escalation path.
- Map plus list view.
- Source label, last checked date, and confidence label on every record.

## Non-Goals

- No private or sensitive personal data.
- No bypassing logins, captchas, access controls, paywalls, or hostile anti-bot systems.
- No claims of completeness where source coverage is incomplete.
- No dependence on one portal or unofficial dataset.
